Support


Product safety

All the products listed on our website is 100% safe to use and has been tested by independent laboratories.


Shipping

We ship worldwide by either Thailand Post (registered airmail ± 6 weeks) or EMS (courier ± 1 week). Tracking numbers will be provided for both shipping options. We will include all product safety certificates in the packages we ship, but customs regulations are different for every country and some countries may not allow the importation of health supplements and/or skincare products. If there is any doubt, it would be best to check with local customs authorities before ordering on this website as we are unable to proccess refunds if an order is confiscated by customs authorities. We regret that we are unable to ship to the following countries:

  • Burkina Faso
  • Eritrea
  • Mauritania
  • Nigeria
  • Sierra Leone
  • Somalia
  • Syria


Returns/Waranty

Although we offer worldwide shipping, postal or courier services might be suspended to certain countries due to civil unrest, conflict or war. If an order has been placed on our website and the selected shipping provider is unable to ship to the destination country, we will issue a full refund for the order amount. Due to the high prevalance of international fraud, we are unable to issue any refunds after an order has been shipped.
