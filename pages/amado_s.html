Amado S Complex Garcinia is a supplement that contains only the best natural herbal extracts. This product helps to reduce and control weight by reducing cravings in-between meals. Amado S Complex Garcinia is manufactured to GMP standards. For best results this product should be used in combination with Amado Detox Fiber.

Key ingredients

Garcinia extract accelerates metabolism and reduces cravings.
Konjac powder helps to keep you feeling full for longer and contains fiber to assist in bowel movements.
Psyllium husk powder relieves constipation, irritable bowel syndrome, and diarrhea
Inulin is a type of water-soluble prebiotic fiber found in onions, leeks, garlic, asparagus, Jerusalem artichoke and many other foods. Prebiotics are indigestible to you, but they help nourish beneficial bacteria in your body.

Dosage
Take 1 capsule per day at bedtime.

This product should not be used by children and pregnant women. People with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), who are more likely to be intolerant to short-chain carbohydrates called FODMAPs, may experience adverse effects from inulin.
